Fibersort® technology

Fibersort® can sort on fiber, color, woven/knitted at 1 pc/s. The Fibersort® is available in two different versions. The concept on the Fibersort® machine is always the same, textiles should be fed one-by-one to the scanners and cameras for most accurate sorting and to enable a single-pass sorting in unlimited categories.
